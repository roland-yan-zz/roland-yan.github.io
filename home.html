Bringing Healthcare to YOU

Quantum computing algorithms, parallel computing, and an integrated mobile & web application will increase diagnostic accuracy, decrease training time, improve ease of usage, and advance existing telemedicine technologies.

Quantm aims to simplify existing approaches to telemedicine by providing an all-in-one utility for both healthcare providers and patients to monitor, track, and consult regarding the progression of an array of conditions.
